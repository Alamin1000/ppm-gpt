Instant answer

Product marketing is a crucial function within a company that focuses on promoting and positioning a specific product or product line to target customers. Its primary goal is to maximize the product's success in the market by creating a strategic plan that encompasses various aspects of the product's lifecycle, from its initial development and launch to ongoing support and growth.

Action items

  • Map out different phases of growth and think about what kind of product marketing team is needed at each phase
  • Get early buy-in from stakeholders and hone in on roles that need to be hired for right away
  • Follow the most money and understand the business goals and levers that will help to achieve them
  • Set two to three OKRs for each product marketer to take on
